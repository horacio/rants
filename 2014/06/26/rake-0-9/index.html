Rake 0.9

Jun 26, 2014 on ruby, tips

Reading bbatsov's blog (Rubocop creator), I found out about something cool introduced on Rake 0.9. Take a look at this:

$ rake -W publish
rake publish  /home/svankmajer/.rvm/gems/ruby-2.1.2@rants/../gh-pages.rake:65:in `<top (required)>'

Yeap, rake -W <task> resolves to where the task is defined. You can also run it without parameters and see that output for all available tasks. Pretty solid.
